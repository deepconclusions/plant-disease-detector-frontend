Team Culture

Once upon a time, in a world where agriculture played a pivotal role in sustaining humanity, a group of diverse and passionate visionaries came together with a shared dream - to revolutionize the way diseases in crops were detected and managed. This group of brilliant minds believed in the power of AI and its potential to shape the future of agriculture.

Led by their visionary CEO, Olimi Expedito, the team embarked on a journey to democratize the power of AI, making it accessible and beneficial for farmers around the world. They were inspired by the promise of the next generation of AI and its potential to transform how we live, work, and interact with our environment.

Guided by their deep convictions and principles, the team set out to create cutting-edge technology that could bring positive change to the lives of farmers and ensure sustainable food production. Their mission was clear - to focus on the needs of their clients, starting with the farmers, and work relentlessly to earn and preserve their trust.

Embracing the LAPs (Landing AI Principles) as their moral compass, the team believed in moving fast to make a real impact. They understood that indecision could be costly, and thus, they prioritized high-speed decision-making and execution, always upholding the highest standards.

To achieve their ambitious goals, the team knew they had to dive deep into understanding the challenges faced by farmers in detecting and managing crop diseases. They held themselves accountable and were not afraid to take on any task that would help them reach their objectives.

Undoubtedly, their journey was filled with challenges, but the team showed true grit and unwavering determination. They faced adversity with firmness of character and an indomitable spirit, consistently inspiring each other to persevere through the tough moments.

In building their technology and company culture, the team placed immense importance on trust. They created an environment of inclusiveness, honest and direct communication, and ownership. Caring for one another, they fostered a culture where constructive criticism was embraced, and humility was valued as much as pride in collective achievements.

The team's hunger for knowledge and their eagerness to solve challenging problems fueled their constant growth and development. They recognized that staying at the forefront of their field required relentless effort and investment in continual learning.

With Olimi Expedito at the helm, supported by talented individuals like Juma Shafara, an Applied ML Engineer, and Twinomugisha Morris, a skilled Data Scientist, the company thrived. They harnessed the power of AI to create a technology that could effortlessly detect diseases in plants, empowering farmers with the tools they needed to protect their crops and ensure bountiful harvests.

Word spread about the company's remarkable achievements, and soon, farmers from all corners of the globe sought their innovative solution to safeguard their livelihoods. Through their unwavering dedication to their founding principles, this diverse and passionate team continued to drive change in the agricultural landscape, making the world a better place, one crop at a time.

Deep Conc-lens Principles

Landing AI Principles (LAPs) are a codification of our way of work. Similar to our DNA, these principles guide us in our work and provide us with a framework for our decision making – be it customer engagement, product development, hiring and talent management, business operations, or sales & support. These principles are intended to align our thought process as leaders of the organization and influence our way of working with people and teams across locations and with businesses.

FOCUS ON CLIENTS

We start with the customer and work backwards to earn and preserve customer trust. While we pay attention to competitors and listen to our advisors, our sole focus is to make our customers successful.

MOVE FAST

We believe in our work’s ability to change human lives. Because the cost of indecision can be more than the cost of a wrong decision, we emphasize high-speed decision making and execution while insisting on the highest standards.

DIVE DEEP

We dive deep to understand the problem, enforce accountability, and drive clarity. We are flexible in using our skills to do whatever it takes to achieve our goal and no task is beneath us.

HAVE GRIT

We exhibit firmness of character and indomitable spirit when faced with challenges. We consistently back ourselves and inspire our teams to power through the low moments.

EARN TRUST

We build an environment of trust. We hyperfocus on inclusiveness, honest and direct communication, ownership, and being vocally self-critical. We care and watch out for each other. We’re humble individually and proud together.

STAY HUNGRY

We are eager to solve challenging problems and acquire new skills. We understand that staying on the bleeding edge is hard work and we are committed to invest in our continual development.
